Benjamin Buhler

Benjamin is a 23 years old designer from Tolochenaz, Switzerland. After studying fashion design in Switzerland, he attended the "École de la Chambre Syndicale de la Couture" during two years in Paris. At the end of his studies, he obtained an internship at the prestigious fashion house Schiaparelli.

Evan Giusto

Evan is a 24 year old designer from Neuchâtel, Switzerland. He studied at the EAA (Ecole d'arts appliqués à La Chaux-de-Fonds (CH)) during four years. After his studies he started working in the watchmaking industry, but continues to pursue his interest in couture as a designer and a model.
